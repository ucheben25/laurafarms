Our Beginning

From a Single Field to a National Platform

Laura Farms was founded in 2012 by agricultural entrepreneur Laura Whitfield on a 200-acre plot in central Nebraska. What began as a family-run operation producing corn and soybeans has grown into one of America's most trusted agricultural investment platforms, managing over 50,000 acres across 7 states.

Our journey was driven by a simple belief: that the wealth created by American farmland should be accessible to everyday investors — not just those with deep pockets or industry connections. Today, over 2,800 investors trust us to grow their capital the way we know best — through the land.
