About Laura Farms

Family Legacy, Modern Agriculture

I started sharing my farming journey on social media in 2020 when I first started renting ground from my grandpa. Since then, I’ve gotten married, graduated college, purchased equipment, toured farms around the world, and educated countless people about the daily wins and challenges of being a farmer.

In addition to farming, I love learning about agriculture input supply, finances, and commodity markets. I am so honored to carry on my family’s legacy. God has given me the opportunity to tell my story, educate others, and now, invite you to invest alongside us!
